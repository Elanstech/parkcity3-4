Living Here

Day-to-day life at Park City 3&4

Your rent includes water, trash collection, access to all amenities (pool, fitness center, parking), 24/7 security, and on-site management services. All utilities are included to make your living experience seamless.

Dogs are permitted with written Board approval (policy in effect since July 2004). All pets must have proper licensing and health certification. Dogs must be on a leash at all times in common areas and must use the basement/service entrance — never the lobby.

No exotic animals or birds are permitted. First violation: $250. Each additional: $500.

The pool is open daily during summer months (May–September) from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM and during winter months (October–April) from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Studio2 persons
1 Bedroom3 persons
2 Bedroom4 persons
3 Bedroom5 persons
Weekday Quiet Hours10:30 PM – 8:30 AM
Weekend Quiet Hours12:00 AM – 8:30 AM

Additionally, all apartments must have 80% floor coverage with rugs or carpeting (except kitchens and bathrooms) to minimize noise transfer.

Guests are always welcome! Short-term visitors don't require notification. For guests staying longer than 7 consecutive days, please notify the management office. Guests may stay no more than 30 days total. Management must be notified in writing.

Violation fine for exceeding guest limits: $1,000/month.

Parking is available for resident shareholders who are current on maintenance. You must register your vehicle with Management (make, model, color, and license plate). A valid vehicle registration and inspection sticker are required at all times.

No vehicle washing or repairs in the garage ($250 fine). No smoking in the garage per NYC law ($250 fine).

Moves & Deliveries

Moving in, out, and receiving large items

For any delivery, move-in, or move-out, management requires:

$500 Retainer Deposit (certified check, bank check, or money order) payable to Park City 3 & 4 Apartments, Inc.
Certificate of Insurance (COI) from the moving/delivery company
If moving yourself: Copy of your current insurance policy

The $500 deposit is refundable after successful completion with no damage.

Permitted Days & HoursMon–Fri, 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM
Schedule in AdvanceMinimum 1 business day
Required EntranceBasement / Service Only
The padded elevator must be used for all moves and deliveries. Non-compliance results in a $500 fine.

A Certificate of Insurance is required for all work, moves, and large deliveries. It must be received at least 2 business days in advance with the following coverage:
