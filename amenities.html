Summer arrives.
The pool opens.

Tucked into the grounds, the seasonal outdoor pool is independently managed and welcomes residents through the warmer months. A quiet ritual that returns each year — sunbeds drawn out, a cool lap before dinner, the long evening light that Queens summers are made of.

Open Memorial Day to Labor Day
Access Residents only · Pass required
Operator Independently managed
I.

At leisure

The pleasures of pause — and a place for the youngest residents.

02

Children's playground

A safe, well-kept playground sits within the grounds — within view of every building, a short walk from any lobby. The kind of small detail that makes the difference for a young family.

  • On the grounds
  • Resident access

03

Park-like grounds

The buildings sit on meticulously maintained gardens — walking paths, mature trees, and the quiet that only comes from grounds tended for seventy years. The "Park" in Park City was always meant literally.

II.

Comings & goings

From the curb to the doorstep, the small thresholds of every day.

04

Two indoor garages

Two 24-hour indoor parking garages serve the community, with assigned spaces — covered, secure, climate-protected. A modest waiting list for new requests; spaces open as residents move on.

05

A nod from the doorman

Each lobby is staffed during the day — the people who know your name, your dog, your delivery schedule. The quiet care you only notice when it's missing, and that residents say keeps them here for decades.

06

Keyless entry

An electronic fob system controls access to every building and both garages. One fob, one key ring — no fumbling at the threshold, no spare key under the mat. Lost fobs are reissued by the management office.

Fob entry · 6 buildings · 2 garages
III.

Always tended

A hand on the building, at all hours.

07

A super in every building

Live-in superintendents reside on-site, available for emergencies after office hours. The kind of arrangement that's grown rare in New York — and that residents have quietly relied on for generations. Familiar faces, not rotating contractors.

  • Live-inin every building
  • Emergenciesat any hour
  • Repairsvia management office
  • Coveragesix buildings, six supers
08

On-site security

Security personnel are on-site to keep watch over the buildings and grounds — a familiar daily presence rather than a distant patrol. Combined with keyless fob entry and lobby staffing, the building feels held without ever feeling guarded.

24-hour presence
IV.

Within reach

The everyday, simplified — never more than a few steps away.

09

Laundry, in every building

A laundry room sits in each of the six buildings — well-kept, conveniently located, never far from your door. No ride to a laundromat, no dragging hampers down the block.

  • One per building
  • Card-operated
  • Resident access
10

Private storage

Personal storage is available in every building, professionally managed by Bargold Storage Systems. Bicycles, holiday boxes, off-season belongings — within steps of your apartment, behind a lock that's yours alone.

Managed by Bargold Storage
