Electric Vehicles Policy

Important safety policy regarding E-bikes and E-scooters

Safety Policy

E-Bikes & E-Scooters Are Banned

Due to the frequency of fires involving electric bicycles and electric scooters, and to ensure the safety and wellbeing of all residents and visitors, E-bikes and E-scooters are banned on all areas of Park City 3&4 Apartments.

This policy was approved by the Board of Directors and incorporates communications from November 23, 2022 and June 20, 2023.

The Ban Includes

  • Storing and charging the battery
  • All coop outdoor spaces (parking garage, walkways, catwalks)
  • Inside residential buildings (common areas, hallways, elevators, apartments)

Guest Policy

Guests of shareholders who bring E-bikes or E-scooters will not be permitted to leave them anywhere on the premises and will be refused entry through the lobby entrances or garages for any duration.

Violation Penalties

Fine per Infringement $150.00
Legal Action May Apply All costs are the shareholder's responsibility
Damage Liability Full Responsibility Shareholder liable for all damage caused

Sublet Policy

Guidelines for shareholders wishing to sublet their apartment

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must own and reside in apartment for minimum 2 years
  • Sub-tenants offered 1-year lease only
  • Subject to interview and written Board approval
  • Renewals require same approval process before expiry
Income Requirement: Sub-tenants must show annual income of 3.5× their annual rent
Important

Sublet Surcharge Fees

Shareholders are charged a surcharge (percentage of maintenance fee) based on how long the unit has been subleased:

Year 1-3 of sublease 15% of monthly maintenance
Year 4 of sublease 30% of monthly maintenance
Year 5 of sublease 50% of monthly maintenance
Year 6+ of sublease 100% of monthly maintenance

Important Notes

  • All apartments must be inspected by Management before subletting
  • Notify Management in writing when subtenant moves out to stop surcharge
  • Policy may be amended at any time by the Board of Directors
Non-Renewal Compliance Fine $500/month Until subtenant is re-certified
Illegal Sublet Fine $1,000 + $500/month Plus legal fees

Tax Abatement

Coop-Condo Property Tax Abatement information for shareholders

Save Money

What is the Coop-Condo Tax Abatement?

The Coop-Condo Property Tax Abatement is offered to NYC residential property owners who use their apartment as their primary residence in order to receive credit or reduce their property tax bill.

The credit is typically applied to the June billing statement (final month of the tax year).

Primary Residence Verification (PRV)

You only need to fill out the PRV form if:

  • Your residency status changed from January 6, 2024 to January 5, 2025
  • You purchased your unit within that time frame
  • Ownership was transferred to a trust or new owner added

Who is Disqualified?

  • Investor-owned apartments (including sponsor units)
  • LLCs, Corporations, and Partnerships
  • Owners of 4+ apartments in same building (unless legally combined)
  • Units receiving J-51, 420c, 421a, 421b, 421g exemptions

Other Available Exemptions

NYC & NYS offer additional exemptions. Apply directly with the City or State:

NYS STAR Credit Senior Citizens' Exemption (SCHE) Disabled Homeowners' Exemption (DHE) Veterans Exemption
