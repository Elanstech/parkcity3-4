Insurance Requirements

Required coverage for all shareholders and sub-tenants at Park City 3&4

Coverage Requirements

All shareholders must maintain a Homeowner's Insurance Policy for the entire time they own their apartment. Sub-tenants must carry Renter's Insurance for their entire lease period.

Liability Coverage $300,000 Minimum required
Personal Property $25,000 Minimum required

Additional Insured Parties

Your policy must list the following as additional insured:

1
Park City 3 & 4 Apartments Inc. Including the Board of Directors
C/O AKAM, 99 Park Ave, 14th Floor, New York, NY 10016
2
AKAM Associates, Inc.
99 Park Avenue, 14th Floor, New York, NY 10016

Submit Your Policy

Please provide a current copy of your insurance policy to the Management Office.

Email To Parkcity3and4@akam.com
In Person Management Office
03

Certificate of Insurance

Required documentation for all contractors, deliveries, and moves

Required

When is a COI Required?

  • All work being done in an apartment
  • All moves (in/out)
  • Large deliveries requiring service elevator
  • Any contractor entering the building
COI must be received at least 2 business days in advance

Required Coverage Limits

Liability Insurance $1,000,000
Product & Completed Operations $1,000,000
Automobile Insurance $1,000,000 Commercial Auto or Hired & Non-Owned
Umbrella Liability $5,000,000 Adjustable based on work scope
Workers Compensation Required Proof must be included

Certificate Holder

All certificates must list the following as certificate holder:

Park City 3 & 4 Apartments Inc. C/O AKAM
99 Park Ave, 14th Floor
New York, NY 10016

Additional Insured Parties

On General Liability and Umbrella Policies:

Park City 3 & 4 Apartments Inc. Including the Board of Directors, C/O AKAM, 99 Park Ave, 14th Floor, New York, NY 10016
AKAM Associates, Inc. 99 Park Avenue, 14th Floor, New York, NY 10016
Resident Name, Unit #, Property Address (If work is for tenant)
Date of Service

How to Submit

Email the COI along with a cover sheet including:

  • Unit owner's name
  • Unit number
  • Description of work/delivery/move
