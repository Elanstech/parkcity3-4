I About

A welcoming home in the heart of Rego Park, since 1955.

Park City 3 & 4 Apartments, Inc. is a well-established cooperative community of six residential buildings, each rising fourteen stories. Every apartment opens onto a private terrace with views of the surrounding neighborhood.

The grounds are meticulously maintained, the buildings carefully tended, and the community looked after by an on-site Management Office and Maintenance team — Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 4 PM — alongside our day-to-day partners at AKAM.

— Park City 3 & 4 Apartments, Inc.

Chapter II — The Buildings

Six buildings.
One quiet idea of home.

Each of our six buildings rises fourteen stories above carefully kept grounds, with full-service lobbies, oversized windows, and a private terrace in every apartment. They share the same bones — and a community spread thoughtfully between 62nd Drive and 63rd Road.
