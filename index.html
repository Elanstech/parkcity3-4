FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about living at Park City

Living Here

Your rent includes water, trash collection, access to all amenities (pool, fitness center, parking), 24/7 security, and on-site management services. All utilities are included to make your living experience seamless.

Yes, we are a pet-friendly community! We welcome cats and dogs with a maximum weight limit of 50 lbs. A pet deposit and monthly pet rent apply. Please contact our office for specific pet policy details.

The pool is open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM during summer months (May-September) and 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM during winter months (October-April).

Payments

You can pay rent through our secure ClickPay portal. Simply log in with your resident credentials and follow the payment instructions. ACH transfers are free, while credit/debit cards incur a small convenience fee.

Rent is due on the 1st of each month. A grace period extends to the 5th of the month. Late fees apply after the grace period ends.

Policies

Guests are always welcome! Short-term visitors don't require notification. For guests staying longer than 7 consecutive days, please notify the management office.

Maintenance requests can be submitted online through your resident portal, by calling the office, or by contacting the on-site superintendent. Emergency maintenance is available 24/7.
